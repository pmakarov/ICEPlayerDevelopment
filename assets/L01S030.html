From the Office of Personnel Management:

The hiring process should begin long before a position is open. The following tips are activities that you should be completing at all times:

  • Ensure position descriptions are systematically reviewed and updated for currency, accuracy and relevance.
  • Keep up a working relationship with your Human Resources Office or representative. Pay attention to announcements and communications from them and interact with them when possible.
  • Keep in touch with your subordinates and peers so you are aware of possible future vacancies.
